topological modular forms

This is a topics course on topological modular forms and related areas of math. We will use the historical development of topological modular forms to guide us through various important concepts in modern algebra, geometry, and topology.

The course syllabus can be found here.

My daily lecture notes are available in the table below. These are full of my accidental mistakes, intentional omissions, and other compromises made to strike a narrative balance for the diverse backgrounds of the 50 registered students. If you happen to catch any mistakes, whether small typos or glaring errors, I'm always grateful for corrections.
