teaching

I enjoy teaching mathematics at all levels, and I consider it an important part of my job as a mathematician. I have designed and taught courses at the middle school, high school, undergraduate, and graduate level, and I have also taught outreach courses for elementary school students. To me, the most rewarding part of teaching is helping students grow in their confidence and passion for math. For more details, see my teaching statement or course evaluations.

I am passionate about mentorship. I run an online mentoring program called Twoples, and I was a project manager for undergraduate research projects in DOmath 2020 and DOmath 2021.
